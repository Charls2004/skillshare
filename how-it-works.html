Step 1: Sign Up & Verify

Getting started with SkillShare™ is quick and secure. Create your account with basic information, then complete our verification process to ensure the safety of our community.

  • Quick account creation
  • Email verification
  • Identity verification
  • Background check
Usually takes 2-3 business days

Step 2: Create Your Profile

Build a compelling profile that showcases your skills and experience. Add photos, certifications, and detailed descriptions to help clients understand your expertise.

  • Professional photo upload
  • Skills and certifications
  • Work experience
  • Portfolio showcase
Complete in 10-15 minutes

Step 3: List Your Services

Create detailed service listings that attract the right clients. Set your pricing, availability, and service descriptions to make your offerings stand out.

  • Service categories
  • Flexible pricing options
  • Availability calendar
  • Photo and video uploads
Set up in 5-10 minutes

Step 4: Connect & Book

Start receiving booking requests and connect with clients. Our secure messaging and payment system makes it easy to discuss requirements and complete transactions.

  • In-app messaging
  • Secure payment processing
  • Booking management
  • Review and rating system
Start receiving bookings immediately
