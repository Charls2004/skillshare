Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SkillShare™?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete the verification process, set up your profile with your skills and experience, and start listing your services. The entire process takes about 15-20 minutes.

Is SkillShare™ free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and listing your services is completely free. We only charge a small commission when you complete a booking, ensuring you only pay when you earn money.

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process includes identity verification with government ID, background checks, and skill validation. This typically takes 2-3 business days and helps ensure the safety of our community.

What types of services can I offer?

SkillShare™ supports a wide range of services including home repair, tutoring, graphic design, photography, consulting, and many more. If you have a skill to share, you can likely offer it on our platform.

How do payments work?

All payments are processed securely through our platform. Funds are held in escrow until the service is completed, then released to you. We support all major credit cards and bank transfers.
